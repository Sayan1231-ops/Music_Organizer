Rules for Uploading Music

  1. Original Content: Only upload music that you own or have permission to distribute. Copyright violations are strictly prohibited.
  2. Audio Quality: Ensure your music is of high quality (minimum 320kbps for MP3 or lossless formats).
  3. Metadata: Include proper metadata, such as the artist name, album title, and genre, to ensure your music is discoverable.
  4. No Explicit Content: Avoid uploading songs with offensive language, hate speech, or any content that violates community guidelines.
  5. File Formats: Supported formats include MP3, WAV, and FLAC. Ensure the file size is under 100MB.
  6. Cover Art: If uploading an album, provide a high-quality cover image (minimum 500x500 pixels).
  7. Spam and Duplication: Do not upload duplicate tracks or spam the platform with multiple versions of the same song.
  8. Platform Rights: By uploading, you grant the platform the right to host, stream, and distribute your music.
  9. Removal Requests: If you wish to remove your music, follow the proper removal request procedures outlined in our terms of service.
  10. Community Respect: Maintain respect for the community and adhere to our platform's policies at all times.
